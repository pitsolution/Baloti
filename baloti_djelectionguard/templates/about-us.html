On our platform you can practice direct democracy in several languages

The baloti.ch. project is carried out by an interdisciplinary team at the Zentrum für Demokratie Aarau in cooperation with the University of Neuchatel. Diversified project partnerships ensure that the project will be made static all over the country.

Voting system

baloti uses a sophisticated voting system to guarantee the security and the transparency of the vote processes. This voting system is provided by Selectio Helvetica.

Selectio Helvetica light

Selectio Helvetica light to safeguard ballot secrecy

The secret ballot protects the voter in a vote or election, so that his or her choice remains confidential. Securing ballot secrecy is a central principle of democratic voting rights. The voting platform baloti.ch uses Selectio Helvetica light (SH light) to meet all the requirements of ballot secrecy. The fact that not even the organizer of the vote is informed of the decision of individual voters is of particular importance.

How Selectio Helvetica light works

Eligible users receive a voting code by email. They then have to enter this code in order for their vote to be counted. The user's Internet browser calculates an encryption of the vote and uses the voting code for an anonymous electronic signature. The encryption and the signature are sent to the electronic ballot. When the polls have closed the correctly signed encryptions are decrypted and sent to the vote organizer. The organizer counts the votes and determines the result of the vote. Ballot secrecy is maintained, as the anonymous signatures provide no clues as to the identity of the individual voter.
